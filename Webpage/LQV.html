Live Quest Visualization

Welcome to the homepage of our Google add-on. This add-on was developed for teachers, who use google forms to create tests for students to assess them. The creator of the form gets an overview of the answers of students for each form as a series of graphs in the Forms menu. The link to the add-on can be found in the Add-on menu, where you can also find the instructions for using it in your form.
